General Questions

What is SocialEQ?

SocialEQ is a low cost fan direct music selling platform. Instead of dragging CDs to your gigs you can now tell your fans to simply tweet a hashtag or post on your Facebook wall to immediately purchase your music.

How does it work?

SocialEQ's magic is in it's simplicity. You create a Facebook post or a tweet in our system. When a fan replies to that post/tweet with a hashtag that was created especially for you we do the rest. An automated reply from your account is sent to the user with a link that they follow to checkout and get their music.

The best part about SocialEQ is that it works on any web enabled device. Fans that are at your live show can now purchase your music in real-time on their phone with minimal hassles.

Sounds like something I should be using, what do I need to sell with SocialEQ?

Getting started on SocialEQ is simple and only requires you to have three things:

  • A Facebook or Twitter account (we highly recommend having both - they are free and offer different promotional value to you as an artist)
  • An email address (we require an email address so we can pay you)
  • The rights to the music you're selling (so your label doesn't try to sue you)

How do I get paid?

SocialEQ will pay you via Venmo to the email address you provide to us when you setup your account. If you need to change the email address, please get in touch with us at support@socialeq.co.

What are the fees?

SocialEQ has the lowest fees in the business - 70% lower than most! You pay only a flat 10% of the selling price when using SocialEQ.

Are there any other hidden fees?

Venmo may charge you an additional fee when you withdrawal your money. We say 'may' as Venmo has a variety of programs and we have no way of knowing which you are in.

I'm already on (iTunes, CDBaby, Bandcamp, etc.), can I still use you?

Yup! SocialEQ is just another tool in your arsenal to promote yourself and your music and we require no exclusivity.

What if I want to give my music away?

We can do that as well. Giving away music has never been easier.

Social Account and Setup Questions

What do I need to be able to sell on Facebook?

To sell on Facebook you need:

  • A personal Facebook account
  • A Facebook Page for yourself / your band
  • An email address (so you can get paid)
  • The rights to sell your music

Once you've got those items you can register right now.

Why do I need to have a Facebook Page?

Facebook's policies dictate that you have a Facebook Page for whichever musician (or record label) if you want to sell. We realize that if you've been on Facebook for awhile you may have started off using your personal page as the touchpoint for your fans as well. We highly recommend creating a Page and promoting it to that audience as Facebook has been pushing for more delineation between personal and professional profiles.

How can a person make a purchase on Facebook?

On Facebook a fan only has to comment on a post with a hashtag you created in SocialEQ to purchase your music. From there SocialEQ will do the rest.

What do I need to be able to sell on Twitter?

To sell on Twitter you need:

  • A Twitter account for your band/label
  • An email address so we can pay you
  • The rights to sell your music

Once you've got those items click hereto get started.

What if I want to sell on Facebook AND Twitter?

Not a problem! Just make sure you've got the above Facebook and Twitter needs mentioned above and click here.

How can a person make a purchase on Twitter?

On Twitter a fan only has to tweet a hashtag @ your twitter account to purchase your music. You can promote these hashtags live in concert, in Youtube videos, in radio interviews and in print. When a user tweets that #hashtag @youraccount SocialEQ does the rest.

Is there another way to get paid besides Venmo?

Not currently. For the simplicity of being able to sign up and receive credit card payments with just an email address and a few pieces of information, we like Venmo.

Technical Questions

I setup an account using my Facebook/Twitter but now when logging in I'm seeing someone else's name.

This happens if you are on someone else's computer and they are logged into Facebook and/or Twitter. What you need to do is log them out of their account(s) and try again using your own.

How do customers get their music?

If a fan on a mobile phone they are able to immediately stream the music on their device. However, due to restrictions in place by Apple and others they can't immediately download the files to their device. To download your music to their device they have to login to SocialEQ from their desktop computer and sync them to their phone from there.

For a fan buying from their desktop computer they just download the files and upload them to the music library management software of their choice.

What music formats do you support?

SocialEQ currently supports mp3, mp4, m4a and m4v files. To keep things simple and compatible with all we recommend using mp3 format.

Can I Include My Album Art With My Digital Campaign?

Due to the way Twitter and Facebook handle photos from a single post we only support one image for the campaign image at this time. However, you can upload the front and back image of the album as part of the digital content so that the user will receive it when downloading the album.

How do I get in touch if I have a question or issue?

You can email support@socialeq.co with any questions or issues you have.
